# Syriatel-Customer-Churn

Business Uderstanding

A Telecom company has been losing its money due to customer churn. The company wants to know what is contributing to the loss and curb it.

Objective

This project aims to determine whether a customer will soon churn Syriatel telecom services.

Key Questions

1.Which feature contributes most to customer churn?

2. Which features are the top 3 contributers of customers churn?

3. Which feature least contributes to customer churn?

Data Understanding 

The data used was obtained from syriatel Telecommunications compan and  contained 3333 rows and 21 columns.


The columns included:

state, account length, area code, phone number,international plan, voice mail plan, number vmail messages,

total day minutes, total day calls, total day charge,total eve minutes, total eve calls, total eve charge,

total night minutes, total night calls, total night charge,total intl minutes, total intl calls, total intl charge,

customer service calls, churn

The dataset did not contain any missing values or duplicates.

The data had class imbalance which was balanced.

Key Findings

Total day minutes is the feature that drives customer churn the most

Total intl calls were the least cause of customer churn.

Having an international plan was a key determinant in determining customer churn.

Total intl minutes and total eve minutes had the same significance in causing customer churn.

Recommendations

1.Come up with loyalty incentives for high usage customers and usage based pricng plans to make their services cost effective to high users.

2.Ensure they have a reasonable cost-benefit international plan

3.Monitor churn trends and adjust pricing or features to address upcoming customer pain points.
